BRGY. NBBS DAGAT-DAGATAN WEBSITE

Additional Info

This is one of my early projects, and I'm proud of how it turned out. The website we created showcases all the important information about Barangay NBBS Dagat-Dagatan, including its history, geography, and COVID cases. Along with my team members Vincent Barcelon, Kim Basada, Roel Dolendo, Mark Lusanta, and Vincent Tayson, we came together as a group called Roborats to develop this site. The purpose behind this project was to provide a detailed and accessible resource for people to learn more about our community, especially during a time when staying informed about local matters, like the impact of COVID-19, is so crucial. We wanted to make sure that the history and geographical significance of Barangay NBBS Dagat-Dagatan were well-represented as well, so everyone can appreciate and understand more about our local area.

MISS PEREGRINE'S HOME FOR PECULIAR CHILDREN

Additional Info

This was one of my early projects, and I really enjoyed working on it. The website we created is all about Miss Peregrine's Home for Peculiar Children. It features chapter summaries, details about the characters, and information about the author, Ransom Riggs. We also included a section that highlights other books written by him. Along with my teammates Vincent, Kim, Roel, Mark, and Tayson, we made this website as part of our group, Roborats. Our goal for this project was to provide a thorough exploration of the book for its readers, offering insights into the characters and the author’s other works. We wanted to create a helpful resource for fans of the book, making it easier for them to dive deeper into the story. It was a great opportunity to blend our passion for literature with web development.

EVERGREEN GENERAL HOSPITAL

Additional Info

This is my current project, and it's been a great experience so far. The goal of this project is to create a more organized and efficient way for the hospital to manage doctor schedules, ensuring that everything runs smoothly for both staff and patients.
